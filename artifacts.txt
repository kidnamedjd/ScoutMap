# Artifact Vault Rules for Scout Map
# Format:
# id | name | weight | description
# weight = relative drop chance (higher = more common).
# Lines starting with # are comments.
#
# Example artifacts:
echo_prism | Echo Prism | 40 | A prism that hums when fog is nearby.
wind_relic | Wind Relic | 25 | A relic that smells of storm air.
star_map | Star Map | 18 | A chart that redraws itself every night.
golden_sigil | Golden Sigil | 10 | A stamped seal of a forgotten guild.
void_compass | Void Compass | 5 | Points to places that don't exist anymore.
