# Expedition Levels for Scout Map
# Format:
# level | rank | xp_to_next
# level starts at 1.
# xp_to_next is the XP needed to reach the next level.
# If a level is missing, the app uses the default XP formula.
# Lines starting with # are comments.
#
# Example levels:
1 | Initiate | 120
2 | Trailblazer | 180
3 | Trailblazer | 260
4 | Stormrunner | 340
5 | Skycartographer | 420
6 | Mythic Pathfinder | 520
